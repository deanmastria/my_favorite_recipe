Directions

How do I make my own delicious donuts?

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Let the dough rise
  3. punch down through the dough to release the air
  4. Roll and cut into doughnuts
  5. Prepare the oil
  6. Fry the doughnuts

Making the glaze

  1. Whisk milk or cream, confectioners sugar and vanilla extract
  2. Dunk each side into the glaze, the glaze will set in about 20 minutes
